Common Questions

How many looks do I get?
Free users get 1 free look. Mane Pro subscribers get 50 looks per month.

Are my photos stored?
No. Photos are processed in real time and never stored on our servers.

Why doesn't the result look realistic?
AI transformations are approximations. Results depend on photo quality, lighting, and angle. For best results, use a well-lit front-facing photo.
